Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Welcome to QR PhotoShare. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the photos you choose to upload for the purpose of sharing them via QR code. We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Photos uploaded to our service are stored temporarily for 12 hours to allow others to download them. After 12 hours, photos are automatically deleted from our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Render: For hosting our backend server.
  • AWS S3: For secure temporary storage of photos.

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) to protect your data during transfer. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
